oneplus: The Best Streaming Platform in Switzerland

oneplus provides international blockbusters as well as original content produced by CH Media's own TV channels. Swiss customers can choose between an ad-supported plan (AVOD) and a paid subscription (SVOD). oneplus is integrated into the most relevant telecommunications and cable providers in Switzerland.

Lovely Systems was chosen as a reliable partner with experience realizing such ambitious plans.

We develop a cross-platfrom web application that runs across web browsers, applications for set-top boxes as well as on big screens like Samsung Tizen.

In the backend Lovely Systems provides services handling catalog data, carrier billing, identity management and much more.
